About us

Tepidem Tech specialises in heating and software solutions to make your life easier.

With decades of cumulated RD and software development experience in the hands of it's employees and owners, the products we aim to bring to the market are built for lasting performance.

Currenlty we are developing a revolutionising system to improve the performance of passive geotermal heating systems, and to bring to market a brand new approach.
