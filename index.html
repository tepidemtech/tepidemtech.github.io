About

Tepidem Tech specialises in heating and software solutions to make your life easier.

With decades of cumulated R&D and software development experience in the hands of it's employees and owners, the products we aim to bring to the market are built for lasting performance.

Currenlty we are developing a revolutionising system to improve the performance of passive geothermal heating systems, and to bring to market a brand new approach.

Why

With the increasing costs of energy and with the current global weather issues,
the need for better, cheaper and eco friendly alternatives is more urgent.
Searching, developing and replacing classical home heating systems
in the 12th hour is not a feasable feat,
so we've embarked early on this jurney to becoming leading experts in
geothermal indoor climate systems using heat exchangers
Currently the water-soil heat exchange systems are very inneficient, and there is a lot of performance left on the table.
In our fight with the ever rising energy prices and CO2 levels, we can not afford half-baked systems with a poor cost to performance ratio.

How

Using our prior experience in the oil&gas industry, we reuse drilling techniques and technologies for a greener future
and our programing skills are directly contributing to monitoring and controlling the system in it's smallest details
We are developing smart monitoring and controlling systems to speed up the transfer of the energy between the liquid traveling
through the soil, and the earth around the tubing.
We improve the performance of the energy storing and retrieval systems, by using better tubing pathways and flow controlls,
that can be achieved with our uniq drilling technique.
